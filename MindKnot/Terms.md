## 6. Multiplayer Features

### 6A. Local Party Mode

Local Party Mode connects devices in the same physical location using
Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework. All game data is transmitted
directly device-to-device. No data passes through our servers. All session
data is ephemeral and is not stored after the session ends.

### 6B. Duel Mode (coming in a future update)

Duel Mode is an asynchronous one-on-one challenge feature. A deep link
(URL scheme: `mindknot://duel/{token}`) is generated and shared by you using your chosen messaging platform. We do not
access or monitor the content of your messages. You are responsible for
choosing who you share a duel link with.

Duel records are stored temporarily in Apple CloudKit and expire 7 days
after creation. Display names you enter for a duel are session names chosen
by you — you are encouraged not to use your real name.

### 6C. Remote Party Mode (coming in a future update)

Remote Party Mode uses Apple CloudKit for real-time game state coordination
across the internet. Room records expire 2 hours after creation. You are
responsible for the display name you enter for each session.

### 6D. Prohibited Conduct in Multiplayer

- Enter offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate display names
- Attempt to intercept, manipulate, or spoof game communications
- Exploit bugs or vulnerabilities in the multiplayer system

We reserve the right to take appropriate action in response to violations,

### 6E. Stats and Session History

The App records your riddle-answering history locally on your device,
including the number of riddles answered, correct answer counts, and
session details (riddle IDs, correctness, timestamps). This data is
displayed to you in the Stats section of the App. No session history is
transmitted to us. If iCloud Sync is enabled (Pro), this data is synced
across your own devices via your private iCloud container as described in
the Privacy Policy.
